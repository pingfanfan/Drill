### Step 2: AI Applies Framework

With materials collected, Claude processes through all 5 layers:

**Layer 1**: Extracts 10-15 evidence points from fetched sources
**Layer 2**: Identifies unspoken consensus about what successful teams know
**Layer 3**: Maps 3 structural assumptions about remote work tools
**Layer 4**: Generates 5 investor-level challenges
**Layer 5**: Synthesizes decision-grade insights

### Step 3: AI Delivers Analysis

Claude outputs structured analysis (see output-examples.md for full example)

## Tips for Best Results

### Be Specific in Your Question

**Better**: "Analyze the opportunity for AI-powered code review tools"
**Less effective**: "Tell me about AI tools"

Specific questions yield more targeted material collection.

### Clarify Your Context

**Helpful context to add**:
- Your target customer segment
- Your current stage (idea, MVP, growth)
- Your constraints (budget, timeline, team)

Example: "We're a 5-person team building for enterprise engineering teams. Analyze the AI code review opportunity."

### Follow Up on Findings

After initial analysis, dig deeper:
- "Drill deeper on Assumption 2—what evidence supports it?"
- "What would make Challenge 4 the most dangerous?"
- "Expand on the user voice—what are the specific complaints?"
